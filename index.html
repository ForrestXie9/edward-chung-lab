Urban Mobility Intelligence Laboratory

Urban Mobility Intelligence Laboratory at The Hong Kong Polytechnic University conducts interdisciplinary research at the intersection of intelligent transportation systems, artificial intelligence, urban computing, and digital twin technologies. The lab develops robust and scalable methodologies for spatiotemporal mobility modelling, uncertainty-aware traffic prediction, adaptive traffic signal control, connected and autonomous transportation, simulation-driven optimisation, and AI-enabled urban decision-making.
