Overview

Welcome to the Urban Mobility Intelligence Laboratory at The Hong Kong Polytechnic University.

Our laboratory is dedicated to advancing intelligent transportation systems and urban mobility through cutting-edge AI and data science methodologies. Led by Prof. Edward Chung, our team comprises 25+ researchers including students, postdocs, and research assistants. Together, we focus on developing algorithms and solutions for AI-enabled transportation systems, digital twin applications, autonomous vehicles, and traffic prediction. Our research interests span a wide range of topics including traffic flow modeling, mobility analysis, smart city solutions, and optimization of urban transportation networks.

We welcome innovative ideas and fresh perspectives from researchers passionate about creating smarter, more sustainable urban mobility solutions.
