Lab Leader

Prof Edward Chung

B. Eng. (Hons), PhD, FIEAust

Prof Chung is Professor of Intelligent Transport Systems at PolyU and an internationally recognised expert in ITS. Prior to joining PolyU, he was the inaugural Director of the Smart Transport Research Centre and Professor of Intelligent Transport Systems at Queensland University of Technology. He holds a Bachelor of Civil Engineering with Honours and a PhD from Monash University.
